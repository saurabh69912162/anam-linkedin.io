Case Study: Agile Transformation at Daimler Trucks

I led the Agile transformation of Daimler Trucks’ SAP S/4HANA procurement project using Scrumban, improving delivery visibility and team synchronization.

  • Introduced sprint reviews and Definition of Done for higher transparency.
  • Used JIRA dashboards for real-time progress tracking and bottleneck resolution.
  • Reduced manual effort through automation and feedback integration.

Result: Predictable delivery cycles, improved cross-team collaboration, and accelerated feature rollout.
